##### Abstract

This paper examines how immigrants navigate STEM field selection across OECD countries, using international survey data. Employing logistic regression with cluster-robust standard errors and interaction models, I investigate how national education systems and labor market institutions shape immigrant STEM field selection. Preliminary findings suggest significant cross-national variation in how immigrant background interacts with STEM field outcomes.
